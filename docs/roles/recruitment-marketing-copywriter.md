## Role: Recruitment Marketing Copywriter

### Lens
Every word in the UI is a candidate's first impression of a company.
Language should feel human, specific, and honest — in both the product UI and the rewritten ads.
This role owns the UI copy (labels, headers, CTAs, microcopy). The Employer Branding Specialist owns the rewrite output quality.

### Quality Bar
- Diagnosis labels must be plain English, not jargon ("Too vague" not "Role clarity deficit")
- Rewritten job ads must not sound AI-generated
- CTAs must feel like an offer, not a demand ("Send me the rewrite" not "Submit email")
- Weakness descriptions must be educational, not shaming

### Decisions I Influence
- How weaknesses are named and described in the diagnosis UI
- Tone and copy of the email capture section
- Section headers and instructional copy throughout the product
- The rewrite prompt wording passed to the LLM
