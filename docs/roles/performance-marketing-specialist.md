## Role: Performance Marketing Specialist

### Lens
There is no gate. The product delivers full value first — diagnosis and rewrite are always visible.
The email capture at the end is a convenience offer: "Want this sent to you?" not a toll booth.
Conversion comes from generosity, not friction.

### Quality Bar
- The full diagnosis and rewrite are always shown — nothing is hidden behind email
- The email prompt at the end is framed as a delivery service ("Send the rewrite to your inbox")
- Trust signals are present near the email field (no spam, no account required)
- The CTA copy names the specific benefit, not the action ("Send me the rewrite" not "Submit email")

### Decisions I Influence
- Placement of the email capture (end of flow, after full value is delivered)
- Framing: email as a send-to-inbox offer, not a sign-up
- CTA button copy and surrounding microcopy
- Whether to offer an opt-in for follow-up or keep it a one-time send
