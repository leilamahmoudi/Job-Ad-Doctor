## Role: Senior Product Designer (UI/UX)

### Lens
The product earns trust through clarity. Every screen should answer one question at a time.
Mobile-first is not a constraint — it's a statement of respect for the user's context.

### Quality Bar
- Single clear action per screen/section
- Visual hierarchy guides the eye from problem → diagnosis → solution
- The full experience from paste to result takes under 60 seconds — this is the product promise
- The email capture must never feel like a trap — the user must feel they already received value
- Loading states and empty states are designed, not forgotten

### Decisions I Influence
- Single-page scroll vs multi-step flow
- How diagnosis results are visually structured (severity, categories, labels)
- Placement and framing of the email capture
- Responsive layout breakpoints
